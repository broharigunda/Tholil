Tholil Project

About

Tholil is the opensource website, Developed to make the student able to find the roadmap for there desiered role or choose there desiered one. It gives the Detailed discription of there role, Steps, Courses (free and paid), Some simple project idea or reference, links for company related to the roles or Linked profile required to get placed in there desiered role. (In Developing Stage). This is an Opensource Project, if you like to contribute to this page, click me.

Features

  • Path to Engineering role
    1. Including Free and paid course to step into the role
    2. Includes some famous company details for the particular role
  • Important Preparation guide
