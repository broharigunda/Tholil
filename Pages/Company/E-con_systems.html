E-Con Systems

E-Con Systems Pvt. Ltd.

Established in 2003, e-con Systems has grown into a leading OEM camera solution provider with a wide global footprint.... They provide end-to-end camera solutions like MIPI camera modules, GMSL cameras, USB 3.1 Gen 1 cameras, stereo cameras, and more. Over the years, They have reimagined how they are used in applications such as retail, medical, industrial, agriculture, smart city, etc. They are also powered by a strong partner ecosystem to offer end-to-end vision solutions, including sensor partners, ISP partners, carrier board partners, etc. So far, They have built 250+ product solutions - shipping over 2 million cameras across 90+ countries around the globe.

Salary: 3LPA to 8LPA (for 2024 batch), but for 2023 batch they offered only 5LPA
Location: Thambaram(MEPZ), Chennai
Ratings: 4.4(on GlassDoor)

What is they working on or service they provide: Manufacturing Camera, Developing AI Models for camera

Topics Need to Cover

For Software:

Basic Aptitude, Advanced Puzzles(logical Reasoning, Analytical), C - Datatype, Operands & Operators, Bitwise Operators, Loops and Branching, Arrays and Strings, Functions, Storage classes, scopes, pointers, Dynamic Memory Allocation, File Operations, Structures and unions, Linked List, Problem Solving

Note:

They expect the more skills in the Problem solving, try to prepare accordingly. If you has the good experience in the hackerrank, leetcode,... etc. You can mention your profile link in the resume. Your Project is very important.

For Hardware:

Basic Aptitude, Advanced Puzzle\s(logical Reasoning, Analytical), Basic Electrical Circuits ( Capacitor, Resistor, VOltage, Power, Inductor, Current), Electrical Laws(ohms, KVL, KCL), Measuring Instruments(Ammeter, Voltmeter), Electron Devices (Diodes, Transistor, FET), Digital Electronics(Multiplexer, Demultiplexer, Flipflops, Logic Gates), Microcontroller and Microprocessor, Analog and Digital Converters, Mesh and Nodal Analysis, Op-Amp Basics

Note:

Project is very important, Prepare well in Microcontroller & Processor (ARM or 8051,8086). Solve more problems in the laws, opAmps, flipflop... etc. They expect you to solve more hardware problems
